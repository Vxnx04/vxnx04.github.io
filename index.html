About Me

Hey there! I'm Vinicius, a Game Designer and Unity Developer who's passionate about creating games that people enjoy. With 2+ years of experience in Unity and C#, I'm constantly learning and growing in this exciting field.

My journey in game development began with a simple belief: games have the power to create meaningful connections with players. I focus on crafting gameplay mechanics and interfaces that are both engaging and accessible, always keeping the player experience at the heart of what I do.

I love the process of bringing game ideas to life, from initial concept to polished gameplay. While I'm still learning and improving every day, I'm committed to creating games that players will enjoy. I value feedback and collaboration, as they help me grow as a developer and create better experiences.
