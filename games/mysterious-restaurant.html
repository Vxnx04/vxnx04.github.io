Development Experience

This project originally began as a trivia-based restaurant game. However, we later pivoted to a match-3 gameplay style to better support the restaurant theme and improve engagement.

  • It was challenging to make the trivia mechanics feel natural within the restaurant setting.
  • When we shifted to match-3, we also introduced a merge mechanic and designed a gameplay loop that alternated between day (action) and night (preparation) phases creating a more dynamic and enjoyable experience for players.
