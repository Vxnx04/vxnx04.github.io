About the Game

This is the alpha version of the game where you'll find pieces of William's mind.

Memories of a Scattered Mind is a Psychological Horror Game that sets you into the shoes of William George Harrison, US Army Vet from the Vietnam War. Following a series of traumatic happenings in his life, along with a poorly treated case PTSD, William has been experiencing strange occurences around his own home.

Development Experience

Memories of a Scattered Mind was my first 3D project in Unity — a psychological horror experience focused on storytelling and atmosphere over traditional jump scares.

  • One of the biggest challenges was sustaining tension and dread through level design, sound, and pacing, without relying on sudden shocks.
  • The decision to avoid jump scares pushed us to carefully craft environmental storytelling and build a slow, immersive narrative around William’s fractured mind.
